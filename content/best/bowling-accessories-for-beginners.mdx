The right accessories solve small problems before they cost frames. Start with maintenance, grip control, and simple transport.

## Keep the ball surface consistent

A microfiber towel and approved bowling ball cleaner help remove lane oil and keep reaction more predictable between games.

## Control grip without guessing

Bowling tape, skin protection, and grip aids can help when hand size changes during a set. Use them conservatively and avoid anything that violates league rules.

## Protect shoes and gear

Shoe covers, a small accessory pouch, and a simple tote or roller bag keep the approach clean and prevent gear from getting damaged in transit.
