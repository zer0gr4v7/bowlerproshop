Cleaner is not magic, but it keeps oil and dirt from hiding what your ball is actually doing on the lane.

## Clean after bowling

Wiping during play and cleaning after the set helps reactive resin maintain a more consistent surface. Follow the cleaner label and league rules.

## Know when cleaner is not enough

If reaction fades after many games, the ball may need oil extraction, surface adjustment, or resurfacing from a pro shop instead of another spray bottle.

## Avoid unsupported claims

Do not expect a cleaner to create hook by itself. It supports the coverstock and surface that are already on the ball.
