A 2-ball bag is the practical middle ground for many league bowlers: one strike ball, one spare ball, and enough room for shoes and accessories.

## Start with transport

If you carry gear across parking lots or long centers, wheel quality and handle stability matter. A cheap bag with weak wheels gets old fast.

## Check shoe and accessory storage

Look for dedicated shoe space and easy-access pockets for tape, towel, cleaner, and small tools. A cramped bag slows down league night.

## Match the bag to your arsenal

A 2-ball roller fits a simple league setup. Tournament bowlers or high-rotation arsenal builders may need a 3-ball or modular setup later.
